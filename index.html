About Us

Designing Spaces
That Tell Your Story

At Kalpvriksh Interior, we believe every space has a soul waiting to be revealed. Based in the heart of New Delhi, our studio brings together artistry, innovation, and meticulous craftsmanship.

Founded in 2015, we have transformed over 500 residences and commercial spaces across Delhi NCR. Our design philosophy is rooted in understanding our client's vision — blending luxury with functionality, tradition with modernity.

From intimate 2BHK apartments to sprawling penthouses and corporate offices, every project receives our undivided attention to detail, premium materials, and a commitment to exceeding expectations.

How We Work

Our Design Journey

A seamless, transparent process crafted to bring your vision to life — from the first conversation to the final reveal.

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with an in-depth conversation to understand your lifestyle, preferences, budget, and aspirations. A free site visit helps us assess the space and its potential.

02

Concept & Design

Our designers craft mood boards, 2D layouts, and photorealistic 3D renderings. Every detail — from color palettes to material selections — is curated just for you.

03

Material Selection

Visit our experience center to touch and feel premium materials. We guide you through the finest options — marble, wood, fabrics, hardware, and lighting fixtures.

04

Execution & Craftsmanship

Our skilled artisans bring the design to life. With dedicated project management and regular updates, you'll stay informed at every stage of the journey.

05

Quality Check & Handover

Thorough quality inspection, final styling, and a grand reveal. We hand over your dream space — polished, pristine, and ready to be lived in.
